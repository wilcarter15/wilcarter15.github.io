Complete Player Program

Group skills training for players of all skill levels grades 2-12! Designed to take players through progressions and repetitions so players build game skills. Speed is a skill we develop and incorporate with all skill work. This class will develop players at any skill level!

Super Select Program

Small group skill training for grades 6-12. This program gives players more repetitions, more attention to details, and more individual coaching from trainers! Training sessions are more intense and more difficult. Players will develop high level ball handling, point guard skills, shooting efficiency, multiple triple threats, passing, footwork, post game, and scoring ability. Speed is a skill we develop and incorporate with all skill work.

Price: $195/month

Schedule

Class trains 2 times a week at the Karl Malone Training Center in Lehi. Space is limited, based on a first come first serve basis. Players will be added to a wait list if class is full. Contact us for registration and availability.
