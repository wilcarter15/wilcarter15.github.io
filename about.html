WHO WE ARE

There are no shortcuts to success! Our trainers help players understand the sacrifice and hard work it takes to succeed on and off the court. Whether you are a starter on your varsity team or picking up a ball for the first time, our training will push players to maximize their potential. We strive to equip players with the fundamental skills needed to play the game. Take your game to the next level!

  • Utah's Best in State Award 2015

Tanner Lind was born and raised in Missoula MT, where he played point guard for Hellgate High School. He attended Utah State University where he was a student assistant for the men’s basketball team from 2002-2005. After Utah State, Tanner coached at Hellgate High School for 5 years before starting Lace ‘Em Up Basketball Skills Training LLC.

In addition to directing his own basketball skills development and training program, Tanner travels extensively to help basketball players all around the world.

Tanner’s Objective: To improve the quality of basketball players by helping players appreciate the effects of positive attitude, work ethic, self-discipline, and self-determination. Lace ‘Em Up! Get in the Game!

Wil Carter

Assistant Trainer

Wil grew up just north of us in Pocatello, ID. He played 2 yrs of juco at SLCC before transferring to San Jose State where he graduated in 2012. Currently, Wil is playing professional basketball overseas and returns in the summers to train with Lace 'Em Up. His coaching and playing philosophy is "Go big or go home"! There is no time for being timid or scared. You have to keep pushing yourself beyond your limits. As a versatile big man, his goal is to use his experience to develop all aspects of a player's game
